About Me

I'm someone with many passions, and technology has always been one of the strongest among them. My journey into programming began when I was 14, during the pandemic, when my curiosity led me to explore the world of code on my own. What started as simple research quickly turned into a deep interest, and from that point on, I've been constantly learning and expanding my knowledge in the field. This passion and dedication eventually led me to be admitted into the second-best university in Latin America and the top tech institution in Brazil , the State University of Campinas, where I continue to refine my skills and grow as a professional.

My love for technology extends beyond just programming. One of my biggest interests is video games—not only playing them but also understanding how they are built. This curiosity naturally led me to explore game development, a field that perfectly combines logic, creativity, and problem-solving. I find it fascinating how games can create immersive experiences that engage players on multiple levels, and I aspire to contribute to that world in my own way.

Beyond my interests in technology and games, I also have a strong passion for volunteer work, something I've been involved in since I was 11 years old. I believe in the power of giving back to the community and making a positive impact on people's lives. My most recent experience in this area was working with Trote da Cidadania, a student-led organization at my university focused on social initiatives. Being part of this project reinforced my commitment to using my skills and time to contribute to meaningful causes.

Another major passion of mine is tabletop RPGs. I love how they bring people together, allowing for incredible storytelling, strategic thinking, and boundless creativity. Whether I'm playing or running a game, I enjoy the process of crafting unique narratives and solving challenges collaboratively.

I'm always eager to learn, experiment, and create. Whether it's developing software, designing a game, or diving into a new project, I thrive on challenges and the opportunity to bring ideas to life. I believe that technology, combined with creativity and social responsibility, has the power to build amazing things, and I'm excited to be part of that journey.
